Founding

Built on energy and trust

Absolute Window Cleaning and Pressure Washing was born from the goal to bring connection, energy, and youthfulness to the St. Louis home service industry.

After successfully operating a previous exterior cleaning partnership (started summer of 2023), I realized that STL Homeowners needed a company that combined energetic service with the reliability of an insured business. I went back to scratch to build Absolute, and here we are!

Questions about us

Everything you need to know about our service, standards, and what makes us different.

How long have you been doing this?

We've been in the exterior cleaning business since summer of 2023, and Absolute was created a year later.

Where is my money going when I hire you?

10% of all profits go back to the community through homeless outreach. We make and keep ziploc bags in the car with materials to immediately help anyone we see on the street, and the rest of that money goes to help buy things for food and clothing drives in our community.

Why should I hire students?

When you hire Absolute, you're hiring a team who makes you the main priority. Because we're not as big as some of our competitors, we won't be taking other work calls while on the job, or shifting your job around on the schedule to accommodate other clients.

Can I let my dog out to say hi when you do the walkaround?

Uh, of course! If it was up to us, dogs would be out the entire time we work, but unfortunately turning a black lab into a golden retriever might not be what you had in mind when scheduling a cleaning.

What happens if something goes wrong during a job?

We're humans, not robots. If we miss a spot or a screen doesn't quite sit right, just tell us! We won't get defensive; we'll get it fixed. Our reputation is worth way more than saving 10 minutes.
